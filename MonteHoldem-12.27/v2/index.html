Opponent Range Definitions

These ranges are a lightweight model implemented in pokerwin/simulator.py (_hand_in_range). They are not full preflop charts—just simple rules.

Tight

  • Pairs: TT+
  • Offsuit or suited: AK, AQ
  • Suited: AJs+ (AJs/AQs/AKs), KQs

Standard

  • Pairs: 77+
  • Suited aces: A2s+ (any suited Ax)
  • Broadways (T/J/Q/K/A): any two broadways if suited; offsuit only AJo+ and KQo
  • Suited connectors: T9s+; suited one-gappers: J9s+

Loose

  • Pairs: any pair
  • Suited: any suited Ax, any suited connector (gap=1), any suited one-gapper (gap=2), or any suited hand with low card ≥ 5
  • Offsuit: ATo+; also two broadways with low card ≥ J (e.g. KJo+)

Random

  • Any two cards (subject to card availability / no duplicates)

Tip: If you want more realistic charts (e.g. 10%/25%/50%), we can refine these rules.
